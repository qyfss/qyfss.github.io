What we do

Our mission

To create opportunities that value and empower young people, build family harmony and foster supportive communities, enabling all young people to reach their full potential.

Our priorities:
  • Protecting charities from abuse or mismanagement
  • Enabling trustees to run their charities effectively
  • Encouraging greater transparency and accountability
